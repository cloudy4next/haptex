Our Services

Private label production

We offer private label production services, where they produce garments for a client's brand under the client's label.

Design and Development

We have in-house designers who can create original designs or work with clients to develop their ideas.

Sourcing & Procurement

We have established relationships with suppliers of fabrics, trims, and other materials necessary for garment production.

Logistics and shipping

We handle the logistics of getting the finished products to the client, whether that involves coordinating with freight forwarders, customs brokers, or other intermediaries.

Quality Control & Assurance

We have dedicated quality control teams that inspect each garment to ensure that it meets the agreed-upon standards for quality, fit, and finish.

Inventory Management & Fulfillment

We offer inventory management and fulfillment services, where we store and manage a client's inventory of finished products and handle the shipping and fulfillment of orders directly to customers.
